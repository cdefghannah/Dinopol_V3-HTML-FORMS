About Culinary Concierge

Learn more about our story and mission.

Culinary Concierge was born out of a shared passion for food, travel, and creating extraordinary culinary experiences. It all started when our founder, Sarah, embarked on a journey to explore the world's diverse cuisines. From the bustling street food markets of Southeast Asia to the Michelin-starred restaurants of Europe, Sarah's culinary adventures ignited a deep appreciation for the power of food to connect people and cultures. Inspired by her experiences, Sarah envisioned a platform that would bring together food enthusiasts, talented chefs, and discerning travelers to create unforgettable gastronomic journeys. In 2010, Culinary Concierge was established with the mission to curate personalized culinary experiences that would tantalize taste buds, foster cultural understanding, and leave a lasting impact on all who partake in the culinary adventures we offer.
